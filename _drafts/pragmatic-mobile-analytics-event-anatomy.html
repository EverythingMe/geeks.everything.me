We've been collecting analytics events ever since we start at 2010. We collected it from various mobile apps in various platforms - iOS, Android, Firefox OS and server-side events.

Fast forward 5 years, we've got a facts table scheme with 200+ columns in our Redshift data store and sub 100 events. This is too much to handle and makes our analytics system less approachable, even if you have re:dash open to the entire company.

[INSERT PROCESS DIAGRAM]

What are we trying to solve here?

  • Reduce the time we spend on defining analytics events for new features.
  • Have a predictable event anatomy we can easily test and communicate.
  • Make our fact table more accessible to non-analysts.
  • Make less mistakes implementing event collection code and detect it earlier.
  • Make analytics collection intertwined rather than stitched to our process.
